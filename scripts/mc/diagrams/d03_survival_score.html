Survival Score — Calculation
Single composite metric · 0-100 scale · combines profitability + risk constraints across 10,000 simulations
SURVIVAL SCORE FORMULA
SurvivalScore = (N_survived / 10000) × 100
where N_survived = # sims satisfying ALL of:
① FinalEquity > InitialEquity (profitable)
② MaxDrawdown ≤ 8% (capital preservation)
③ Sharpe ≥ 1.0 (risk-adjusted floor)
A simulation "survives" only if it remains profitable AND within risk constraints. The 8% MDD threshold (vs 5% live target) gives 60% headroom for Monte Carlo's stress conditions. Below 95% = strategy is fragile to the 3 randomization dimensions.
