Pass / Failure Criteria
12 hard rules (5 critical + 5 major + 2 minor) · 3-band certification verdict · Survival Score floor is non-negotiable
PASS Criteria — CERTIFIEDLIVE OK
PASS-01
Survival Score ≥ 95% — at least 9,500 of 10,000 simulations remained profitable AND within risk constraints (MDD ≤ 8%, Sharpe ≥ 1.0).
PASS-02
P5 Sharpe ≥ 1.0 — the worst 5% of simulations still achieve Sharpe ≥ 1.0 (institutional floor).
PASS-03
P5 Max Drawdown ≤ 8% — the worst 5% of simulations stay within 8% drawdown (60% headroom over 5% live target).
PASS-04
P5 CAGR ≥ 10% — the worst 5% of simulations still return ≥ 10% annualized (positive real return).
PASS-05
Risk of Ruin < 1% — fewer than 100 of 10,000 simulations lose ≥ 50% of capital.
PASS-06
P5 Final Equity > 1.0× — even the worst 5% of simulations end with more capital than they started.
PASS-07
Spread sensitivity low — Survival Score at 2× baseline spread drops by < 5 percentage points.
FAILURE Criteria — REJECTEDVETO
CRIT-01
Survival Score < 85% — more than 15% of simulations fail. Strategy is fragile across the 3 randomization dimensions.
CRIT-02
P5 Sharpe < 0.8 — worst 5% of sims are no better than buy-hold. Strategy has no edge in adverse conditions.
CRIT-03
P5 MDD > 10% — worst 5% of sims exceed 10% drawdown (2× the 5% live floor). Capital preservation fails.
CRIT-04
Risk of Ruin ≥ 5% — more than 500 of 10k sims lose ≥ 50% of capital. Catastrophic tail risk.
CRIT-05
Negative P5 CAGR — worst 5% of sims lose money over the period. Edge does not survive randomization.
MAJ-01
Survival Score 85-94% — moderate fragility. Paper trading only with reduced position sizing.
MAJ-02
P5 Sharpe 0.8-0.99 — borderline edge in worst 5%. Conditional approval with monitoring.
MAJ-03
P5 MDD 8-10% — exceeds live target in worst 5%. Tighter risk controls required.
MAJ-04
Spread sensitivity high — Survival Score drops > 10 pp at 2× baseline spread. Strategy is spread-dependent.
MAJ-05
Slippage sensitivity high — Survival Score drops > 10 pp at P99 slippage on all trades.
MIN-01
P5 CAGR 5-10% — marginal return in worst 5%. Advisory only.
MIN-02
Risk of Ruin 1-5% — small but non-negligible tail. Monitor.
CERTIFICATION GATES — 3-BAND DECISION
Applied after all 10,000 simulations complete
CERTIFIED
Score ≥ 95 · P5 Sharpe ≥ 1.0 · P5 MDD ≤ 8% · RoR < 1%. Live trading authorized. Quarterly re-MC.
CONDITIONAL
Score 85-94 OR P5 Sharpe 0.8-0.99 OR P5 MDD 8-10%. Paper only. 30-day re-MC required.
REJECTED
Score < 85 OR P5 Sharpe < 0.8 OR P5 MDD > 10% OR RoR ≥ 5%. Trading HALTED. Engineering review.
