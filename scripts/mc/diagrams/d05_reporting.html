Reporting System & Worked Example
3-tier reporting · S3 archived 7 years · distribution histograms visualize strategy robustness
REPORTING PIPELINE
Generation · Archive · Dispatch
ARCHIVE
All reports to s3://titan-mc/{strategy}/{version}/{timestamp}/ · 7-year retention · RSA-2048 signed manifest
DISPATCH
PagerDuty (P1 for REJECT, P3 for PASS) · Slack #titan-mc · Email to stakeholders · Live dashboard
VERSIONING
4-tuple: strategy + data + cost-profile + engine + seed. Full reproducibility — same tuple = identical sims.
COMPARISON
Each MC compared against last 5 MC runs of same strategy. > 5 pp Survival Score drop = regression P1 alert.
CADENCE
Pre-deployment (mandatory) · Quarterly scheduled · On-demand via CLI / REST
EXECUTIVE
1-page brief
Verdict, Survival Score headline, P5/P50/P95 metrics, distribution histogram thumbnail, comparison to last 5 MC runs.
PDF · 1 page
TECHNICAL
Full sim dump
All 10k sim results, per-dimension sensitivity, equity curve samples, percentile distributions, parameter robustness.
PDF + JSON · 15-25 pp
REGULATORY
Audit trail
Random seed, methodology, distribution parameters, reproducibility manifest, sign-off chain.
PDF · 8-12 pp

REPORT CONTENTS — TECHNICAL TIER (FULL DETAIL)
SECTION 1
MC configuration — sim count (10,000), seed, randomization dimensions enabled, distribution parameters per dimension.
SECTION 2
Percentile distribution table — P5/P25/P50/P75/P95 for Sharpe, MDD, CAGR, Final Equity, Profit Factor, Recovery Factor.
SECTION 3
Survival Score breakdown — total sims, sims profitable, sims within MDD, sims within Sharpe, sims meeting all 3.
SECTION 4
Per-dimension sensitivity — Survival Score when only trade-order shuffled, only slippage randomized, only spread randomized. Isolates fragility source.
SECTION 5
Equity curve samples — 5 sample equity curves (P5, P25, P50, P75, P95) for visual inspection of path-dependency.
SECTION 6
Histograms — Sharpe distribution, MDD distribution, Final Equity distribution (10 bins each).
SECTION 7
Certification verdict — 3-band decision, Survival Score, hard veto triggers fired (if any), waiver IDs (if any).
