Design Patterns: Strategy (IWeightingAlgorithm — 4 interchangeable algorithms), Observer (WeightingEngine subscribes to trade outcomes + CEO directives), Factory (AlgorithmFactory creates algorithm from config), Meta-Strategy (MetaBandit selects strategy). All classes fully typed (mypy --strict). Zero GPU dependency — all computation is NumPy vectorized. Zero external service calls — fully offline. The MetaBandit is the key innovation: it treats algorithms themselves as bandit arms, automatically selecting the best algorithm per regime without human tuning.
