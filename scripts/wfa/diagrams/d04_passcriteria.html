Pass Criteria — Hard Veto Triggers
8 critical · 6 major · 3 minor · WFE floor is non-negotiable (overfitting canary)
PASS CriteriaCERTIFIED
WFE
WFE ≥ 0.50 — out-of-sample Sharpe is at least 50% of in-sample Sharpe. Indicates the strategy generalizes; not overfit.
OOS SHARPE
All 5+ folds OOS Sharpe ≥ 1.5 — every fold must independently clear the institutional Sharpe threshold.
OOS MDD
OOS Max Drawdown ≤ 12% — aggregate OOS drawdown across all test folds stays within capital-preservation limits.
FOLD CONS
Fold consistency ≥ 0.80 — at least 80% of folds are profitable (4 of 5, or 6 of 7). No catastrophic single-fold failures.
OOS CAGR
OOS CAGR ≥ 25% — post-cost annualized return on out-of-sample data. Below 25% means marginal edge after costs.
COST DRAG
OOS Cost drag ≤ 35% — strategy retains at least 65% of idealized edge in live-like conditions.
AGG SCORE
Aggregate score ≥ 80 — weighted blend of all sub-metrics (WFE has 30% weight).
NO OVERFIT
Parameter stability — selected parameters vary by < 30% across folds (no parameter-chasing).
Fail / Veto CriteriaREJECTED
CRIT-01
WFE < 0.35 — severe overfitting. OOS performance is < 35% of IS performance. Strategy does not generalize.
CRIT-02
Any fold OOS Sharpe < 1.0 — at least one fold is no better than buy-hold. Strategy has a regime it cannot handle.
CRIT-03
OOS MDD > 15% — capital preservation failure in OOS data. Risk controls failed under realistic conditions.
CRIT-04
Negative OOS CAGR — strategy loses money out-of-sample. Fundamental flaw.
CRIT-05
Parameter collapse — best parameters vary > 100% across folds (e.g., 20-period in fold 1, 50-period in fold 2). Optimization is fitting noise.
CRIT-06
Data leak detected — automated scanner finds any in-sample data used in OOS test (lookahead bias, survivorship, etc.).
MAJ-01
WFE 0.35-0.49 — moderate overfitting. Strategy may still work but requires reduced position sizing.
MAJ-02
1 fold OOS Sharpe 1.0-1.49 — one weak fold (others OK). Strategy has a marginal regime.
MAJ-03
Fold consistency < 0.60 — too many losing folds. Strategy is not robust.
MAJ-04
Aggregate score 65-79 — borderline. Paper trading required before live capital.
MIN-01
OOS CAGR 20-25% — marginal return. Advisory only.
MIN-02
Cost drag 35-45% — high cost sensitivity. Monitor broker cost drift.
MIN-03
Fold count < 5 — insufficient sample. Re-run with longer dataset.
CERTIFICATION GATES — 3-BAND DECISION
Applied after all folds complete · Aggregate score + WFE + fold-level checks
CERTIFIED
Score ≥ 80 · WFE ≥ 0.50 · all folds OOS Sharpe ≥ 1.5 · MDD ≤ 12%. Live trading authorized. Quarterly re-WFA.
CONDITIONAL
Score 65-79 OR WFE 0.35-0.49 OR 1 weak fold. Paper trading only. 30-day re-WFA required.
REJECTED
Score < 65 OR WFE < 0.35 OR ≥ 2 weak folds OR MDD > 15%. Trading HALTED. Engineering review.
