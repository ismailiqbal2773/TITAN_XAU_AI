Scoring System — WFE & Aggregate Score
Walk-Forward Efficiency (OOS / IS Sharpe) is the headline metric · Aggregate score is weighted across 6 sub-metrics
SCORING FORMULAS
WFE = SharpeOOS,median / SharpeIS,median    |    Target: ≥ 0.50 (50%)
AggregateScore = (30% × WFE_norm) + (25% × OOS_Sharpe_norm) + (15% × OOS_MDD_norm) + (15% × OOS_CAGR_norm) + (10% × Fold_Consistency) + (5% × Cost_Drag_norm)
All sub-metrics normalized to [0, 100] using min-max scaling against target bands. WFE ≥ 0.50 required for PASS — it is the single most important metric (anti-overfitting canary).
