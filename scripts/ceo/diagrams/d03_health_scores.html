6 Health Scores — Composition & Thresholds
5 sub-scores → 1 overall · each 0-100 · GREEN/YELLOW/RED thresholds per score
SCORE-1 · MODEL HEALTH
0-100
Per-Model Health Score
Per model (XGBoost/LSTM/Transformer/RL). Computed from WR, PF, Sharpe, Sortino, MDD, Recovery across 4 rolling windows. Detects degradation, drift, overfitting, instability.
Formula: 0.25×Sharpe_norm + 0.20×Sortino_norm + 0.20×PF_norm + 0.15×WR_norm + 0.10×Recovery_norm + 0.10×(1-MDD_norm)
≥85 GREEN
70-84
<70 RED
SCORE-2 · EXEC QUALITY
0-100
Execution Quality Score (EQS)
System-wide. Computed from latency P99, slippage P50/P90/P99, fill rate, requote rate. Detects execution deterioration, broker-side issues.
Formula: 0.30×(1-Latency_norm) + 0.30×(1-SlipP50_norm) + 0.20×(1-SlipP99_norm) + 0.20×FillRate
≥85 GREEN
70-84
<70 RED
SCORE-3 · RISK
0-100
Risk Score (lower=better, inverted)
System-wide. Inverted: 100=safe, 0=ruin. From MDD (live), exposure, margin level, per-trade risk utilization, correlation. Detects risk buildup before breach.
Formula: 100 - (0.40×MDD_pct + 0.20×Exposure_pct + 0.20×(100-MarginLevel) + 0.20×RiskUtil)
≥85 GREEN
70-84
<70 RED
SCORE-4 · BROKER QUALITY
0-100
Broker Quality Score (BQS) — per broker
Per broker (6 brokers). Computed from spread deviation, slippage vs baseline, fill rate, reconnect frequency, server stability. Detects broker-specific issues.
Formula: 0.25×(1-SpreadDev) + 0.25×(1-SlipDev) + 0.25×FillRate + 0.25×(1-ReconnectRate)
≥85 GREEN
70-84
<70 RED
SCORE-5 · REGIME CONF
0-100
Regime Confidence Score
System-wide. From regime detector's 3-model vote agreement, transition confidence, regime-specific strategy performance. Detects regime ambiguity + regime-specific failures.
Formula: 0.40×VoteAgreement + 0.30×TransitionConf + 0.30×RegimeStrategyPerf
≥85 GREEN
70-84
<70 RED
SCORE-6 · SYSTEM HEALTH
0-100
Overall System Health Score
Aggregates 5 sub-scores (worst-of-model-health weighted highest) into single number. Drives GREEN/YELLOW/RED system status.
Formula: 0.30×min(ModelHealth) + 0.25×RiskScore + 0.20×EQS + 0.15×RegimeConf + 0.10×min(BQS)
≥85 GREEN
70-84
<70 RED
OVERALL SYSTEM HEALTH — WEIGHTED AGGREGATION
min(ModelHealth) weighted highest — a single failing model can drag down overall health
OverallHealth = 0.30 × min(ModelHealth[i] for i in models) # weakest model dominates
+ 0.25 × RiskScore # capital preservation is critical
+ 0.20 × ExecutionQualityScore # fills matter
+ 0.15 × RegimeConfidenceScore # regime ambiguity reduces confidence
+ 0.10 × min(BrokerQualityScore[b] for b in brokers) # worst broker caps
