Decision Layer & Control Actions
3-band system status → 5 control actions · automated · audited · reversible (except emergency)
GREEN STATUS — System Healthy
CRITERIA
OverallHealth ≥ 85 AND all sub-scores ≥ 80 AND 0 critical detectors firing
MODEL INFLUENCE
Normal weights — ensemble voter uses design weights (25% each)
RISK MODE
Normal — full position sizing, standard stops
TRADING
Live trading authorized — all strategies active
ALERTS
P3 informational · Slack #titan-ceo only
YELLOW STATUS — Caution / Degrading
CRITERIA
OverallHealth 70-84 OR any sub-score 70-79 OR 1+ detectors firing (non-critical)
MODEL INFLUENCE
Reduced 25-50% on flagged models · ensemble voter downweights
RISK MODE
Defensive — position size ×0.75, stops widened 1.25×
TRADING
Live trading continues at reduced size · no new strategies activated
ALERTS
P2 · PagerDuty + Slack · 15-min re-evaluation
RED STATUS — Critical / Emergency
CRITERIA
OverallHealth < 70 OR RiskScore < 70 OR any critical detector firing OR kill-switch triggered
MODEL INFLUENCE
Failing models disabled · ensemble may operate with 2/4 models if quorum still met
RISK MODE
Emergency — positions flattened <500ms · capital preservation mode 24h
TRADING
HALTED — no new entries · existing positions managed defensively
ALERTS
P1 · PagerDuty immediate · CTO + Risk Officer paged
5 Control Actions
ACTION-1
YELLOW
Reduce Model Influence
Downweight flagged model in ensemble voter by 25-50%. Reversible when score recovers.
Effect: ensemble weight × 0.5-0.75 · quorum re-evaluated
ACTION-2
GREEN
Increase Model Influence
Restore weight to design value after recovery. Staircase: 50%→75%→100% over 3 cycles.
Effect: weight restored · 3-cycle staircase verification
ACTION-3
RED
Disable Failing Model
Hard-disable model in ensemble. Requires manual re-enable after review. Quorum check.
Effect: model weight = 0 · quorum drops to 3/4 · manual re-enable required
ACTION-4
RED
Emergency Risk Reduction
Trigger risk engine emergency mode: flatten positions <500ms, halt new entries, 24h protective.
Effect: risk mode = EMERGENCY · flatten <500ms · 24h protective
ACTION-5
RED
Capital Preservation Mode
Sustained RED (>30min): flatten all + 24h no-trade. Resume only after manual sign-off.
Effect: all flat · 24h no new entries · manual resume required
