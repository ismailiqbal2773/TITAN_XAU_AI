Realistic Cost Model — Per-Trade Attribution
5 cost components applied to every simulated fill · Separates idealized P&L from realistic P&L · Surfaces cost drag as a first-class metric
REALISTIC P&L FORMULA (PER TRADE)
RealizedPnL = (ExitPrice − EntryPrice) × Direction × Lots × 100 oz
− SpreadCost − Commission − Swap − SlippageCost
SpreadCost = Spreadentry × Lots × 10 USD/pt + Spreadexit × Lots × 10 USD/pt
Commission = Lots × RateRT (USD/lot, broker-specific)
Swap = Σ Notional × Ratedaily × 1/365 (overnight days held)
SlippageCost = Lots × 100 × |FillPrice − SignalPrice|
All components in USD. Lot = 100 oz XAUUSD. Direction: +1 long, −1 short. Triple swap on Wednesdays for positions held overnight.
