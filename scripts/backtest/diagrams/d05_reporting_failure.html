Reporting System & Failure Criteria
3-tier report distribution · 3-band certification · 10 critical/major/minor failure rules
REPORTING SYSTEM
3-Tier Report Distribution
ARCHIVE
All reports archived to S3://titan-backtests/{strategy}/{version}/{timestamp}/ with 7-year retention. RSA-2048 signed manifest.
DISPATCH
Auto-dispatched via PagerDuty (engineering), Slack #titan-backtests (ops), email (stakeholders). Live dashboard at https://titan.io/backtests.
VERSIONING
Every backtest pinned to: strategy version + data version + cost-profile version + engine version. Full reproducibility.
COMPARISON
Each run compared against last 5 runs of same strategy. Regression detection: > 15% score drop = P1 alert.
EXECUTIVE
1-page brief
Verdict, headline metrics (Sharpe/MDD/CAGR), cost drag, regime breakdown, comparison to benchmark. For CTO / portfolio manager.
PDF · 1 page
TECHNICAL
Full metrics dump
All 24 metrics, per-trade ledger, equity curve, drawdown profile, regime attribution, cost breakdown, parameter sensitivity.
PDF + JSON · 15-30 pp
REGULATORY
Audit trail
Data lineage, methodology, assumptions, cost calibration evidence, reproducibility manifest. For compliance / external audit.
PDF · 8-12 pp
FAILURE CRITERIA
3-Band Verdict + Hard Veto Triggers
PASS · CERTIFIED
All metrics in target band. Cost drag ≤ 35%. Realistic CAGR ≥ 35%. MDD ≤ 12%. Sharpe ≥ 2.0. 0 critical failures.
CONDITIONAL
1-2 metrics outside target, no critical failures. Strategy approved for paper / small-capital live with daily revalidation. Re-backtest in 7 days.
REJECT · VETO
Any critical failure, or > 2 major failures, or aggregate score < 65. Strategy not approved for live capital. Engineering review required.
HARD VETO TRIGGERS (10)
CRIT-01
Sharpe < 1.5: insufficient risk-adjusted return. Strategy is no better than buy-hold.
CRIT-02
MDD > 20%: unacceptable drawdown. Capital preservation failure.
CRIT-03
Cost drag > 50%: more than half of edge lost to costs. Strategy is unviable.
CRIT-04
Negative CAGR: strategy loses money over 12 months. Fundamental flaw.
CRIT-05
Lookahead bias detected: strategy uses future data. Critical methodological error.
MAJ-01
Profit factor < 1.3: marginal edge, vulnerable to cost drift.
MAJ-02
Win rate < 45%: low hit rate, depends on outsized winners (fragile).
MAJ-03
Regime concentration > 70%: profit from 1 regime only. Not robust.
MAJ-04
Trades < 200 in 12 months: insufficient sample for statistical confidence.
MIN-01
Calmar < 2.0: drawdown-to-return ratio suboptimal. Advisory only.
