Final Certification Workflow
7-step pipeline · 3 decision gates · 4-role sign-off chain · End-to-end runtime < 2 minutes
STEP 01
Pre-flight Check
Verify environment: VPS connectivity, MT5 terminal running, license JWT valid, network latency < 100 ms.
env_ok
→
STEP 02
Suite Registry Load
Initialize 8 suite descriptors, load 144 check definitions from spec/manifest.yaml, resolve rule engine.
registry_ready
→
STEP 03
Parallel Suite Execution
Run all 8 suites concurrently via asyncio. Each suite reports its own pass/fail/score with sub-30s timeout.
8 results
→
STEP 04
Rule Engine Evaluation
Apply severity gates: critical veto, major caps, minor thresholds. Compute per-suite score and aggregate.
score + verdict
→
STEP 05
Stability Verification
Repeat full sweep 3 times, 60 s apart. If any run differs from others → flaky → veto. Confirm 3 stable PASS.
stable_pass
→
STEP 06
Certification Decision
Apply 3-band gate: CERTIFIED / CONDITIONAL / REJECTED. Generate certification report PDF + JSON manifest.
cert_report
→
STEP 07
Sign-off & Dispatch
4-role digital sign-off (Eng / Risk / Compliance / CTO). Dispatch to PagerDuty, S3 archive, trading gate.
TRADE_AUTH
CERTIFIED · GREEN
Trade Authorized
Score ≥ 85 · 0 critical · ≤ 6 major · 3 stable runs. Trading gate opens. Revalidation cadence: every 7 days OR on any module hot-reload.
CONDITIONAL · AMBER
Trade with Daily Recheck
Score 70–84 OR 1 critical with documented waiver. Trading authorized for 24 h. Mandatory revalidation every 24 h. CTO sign-off required.
REJECTED · RED
Trade Halted
Score < 70 OR ≥ 2 critical OR flaky. Trading gate locked. Existing positions flattened per risk engine. Engineering escalation within 1 h.
