Pass / Fail Rules Engine
Severity-gated criteria · Critical = hard veto · Major = score deduction · Minor = advisory
PASS Criteria ALLOW
CRITICAL
0 critical failures across all 8 suites. Any critical failure triggers immediate veto regardless of overall score.
MAJOR
Major failures ≤ 2 per suite, and total major failures ≤ 6 across all suites. Each excess major failure deducts 3 points from suite score.
MINOR
Minor failures allowed up to 4 per suite. Each excess minor failure deducts 0.5 points from suite score.
SCORE
Weighted aggregate score ≥ 85/100 across all 8 suites. Suite-level minimum: 75/100 for each suite.
LATENCY
Full validation sweep completes within 90 seconds on VPS. Per-suite timeout 12 seconds with graceful degrade.
STABILITY
3 consecutive validation runs all PASS, run 60 seconds apart. Eliminates transient flakiness.
FAIL Criteria VETO
CRITICAL
Any critical check fails → automatic veto. Score cannot exceed 49 regardless of other results. Suite is marked UNFIT.
SCORE
Weighted aggregate score < 70 → CERTIFICATION REJECTED. Score 70–84 → CONDITIONAL (retest within 24 h required).
SUITE
Any suite scoring < 60 → automatic overall veto. The system cannot trade with any failing subsystem.
MAJOR
Total major failures > 10 across all suites → veto. Per-suite major > 4 → suite veto.
LATENCY
Suite execution exceeds 30 s timeout → suite marked DEGRADED, score capped at 65.
FLAKY
3 consecutive runs cannot reach stable PASS → SYSTEM UNSTABLE veto. Requires engineering review.
CERTIFICATION GATES
Three-tier decision matrix applied after all suites complete
CERTIFIED
Score ≥ 85, 0 critical, ≤ 6 major, 3 stable runs. Trading authorized. Re-validate every 7 days.
CONDITIONAL
Score 70–84 OR 1 critical with documented waiver. Trading authorized with daily revalidation. Expires in 24 h.
REJECTED
Score < 70 OR ≥ 2 critical OR unstable. Trading HALTED. Engineering escalation required.
