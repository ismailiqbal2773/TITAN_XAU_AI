CERTIFICATION PIPELINE
6 Scenarios → Aggregate → Verdict
AGGREGATION
Final verdict = logical AND of 6 scenario verdicts. If ANY scenario = REJECTED → final = REJECTED.
CERTIFIED
All 6 scenarios PASS · 0 critical · 0 major · kill-switch SLA met · 100% position reconciliation
CONDITIONAL
1 major failure OR 1 critical with documented waiver · paper trading only · 30-day re-stress
REJECTED
Any critical (no waiver) OR ≥ 2 major · trading HALTED · engineering review required
ARCHIVE
s3://titan-stress/{strategy}/{version}/{timestamp}/ · 7-year retention · RSA-2048 signed manifest
DISPATCH
PagerDuty P1 for REJECT · P3 for PASS · Slack #titan-stress · email to stakeholders
CADENCE
Pre-deployment (mandatory) · Quarterly scheduled · On-demand via CLI / REST
REGRESSION
Each stress test compared to last 5 · any scenario downgrade = P1 regression alert

REPORT CONTENTS — TECHNICAL TIER (FULL DETAIL)
SECTION 1
Test configuration — strategy version, dataset, seed, scenario parameters, broker profile.
SECTION 2
Per-scenario results — for each of 6 scenarios: metric value, threshold, pass/fail, tick-level equity curve, recovery actions taken.
SECTION 3
Kill-switch audit — every kill-switch trigger with timestamp, latency, positions flattened, audit hash.
SECTION 4
Recovery timeline — DETECT → HALT → FLATTEN → PROTECT → RECOVER → RESUME with timestamps and durations.
SECTION 5
Failure analysis — for any failed scenario: root cause, dimension of failure (kill-switch / filter / sizing / reconnect).
SECTION 6
Certification verdict — 3-band decision, aggregate score, hard veto triggers fired, waiver IDs (if any).
