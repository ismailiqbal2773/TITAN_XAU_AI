Failure Logic — Hard Veto Triggers
12 rules (5 critical + 5 major + 2 minor) · any 1 critical OR any 2 major = automatic REJECT
CRITICAL Failures (5)AUTO-VETO
CRIT-01
Flash crash MDD > 12% — kill-switch failed to flatten in time, or stop-loss fills slipped beyond threshold. Capital preservation failed.
CRIT-02
Kill-switch latency > 500 ms — emergency flatten exceeded SLA. Critical risk control non-functional.
CRIT-03
Phantom orders after disconnect — position reconciliation failed. Local state ≠ broker state. Trading must halt.
CRIT-04
Gap loss > 2% equity — weekend position policy failed. Strategy held exposure through gap, lost >2%.
CRIT-05
Stale fill executed — signal veto failed, order placed on stale data. Could cause catastrophic loss.
MAJOR + MINOR Failures (7)CONDITIONAL
MAJ-01
Cost drag > 45% during high spread — strategy entered trades during widened spread despite news filter.
MAJ-02
Stale veto rate < 95% — risk engine allowed > 5% of stale signals through during lag scenario.
MAJ-03
Vol spike MDD > 10% — vol regime detection failed to reduce size, drawdown exceeded threshold.
MAJ-04
Reconnect > 5 seconds — broker reconnection logic too slow. Increased exposure window.
MAJ-05
Recovery > 30 min — system failed to auto-recover. Required manual intervention.
MIN-01
Cost drag 35-45% — elevated but within tolerance. Monitor.
MIN-02
Stale veto 90-95% — borderline. Investigate veto logic.
CERTIFICATION GATES — 3-BAND DECISION
Applied after all 6 scenarios complete · aggregate verdict = logical AND of all 6
CERTIFIED
All 6 scenarios PASS · 0 critical · 0 major. Live trading authorized. Quarterly re-stress.
CONDITIONAL
1 major failure OR 1 critical with waiver. Paper only. 30-day re-stress required.
REJECTED
Any critical (no waiver) OR ≥ 2 major. Trading HALTED. Engineering review.
